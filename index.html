Otonabee Minor Soccer

Fun, affordable youth soccer for everyone.

Otonabee Minor Soccer Association is a youth soccer league based in Keene, Ontario. Powered by amazing volunteers, we strive for a fun, friendly atmosphere that rewards teamwork and fair play.
